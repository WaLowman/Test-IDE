## 2. The Concept

**Authentic Cultural Experience Packages (ACEs)** - Short, curated cultural excursions for overseas business and government visitors to Johannesburg.

**The Opportunity:**
- Johannesburg is not a major tourist destination BUT receives many international business visitors
- These visitors often have gaps in their schedules (evenings, half-days between meetings)
- The G20 Summit in November 2025 brought tens of thousands of delegates, staff, and associated visitors to Johannesburg, which sharpened the team's thinking about a premium cultural offer for time-poor business and government travellers
- Business travellers want authentic local experiences, not generic tourist fare

**The Concept:**
Extend the existing ground transportation business to offer curated cultural experiences that showcase authentic Johannesburg—its heritage, food, art, and communities—to time-poor business visitors. The idea gained real momentum during the run-up to the G20 Summit in 2025, when the team recognised how many international visitors needed high-quality, well-managed experiences in limited time windows.

## 9. Additional Context for Realistic Exercises

### Why This Could Work
- Existing transport infrastructure and driver relationships
- Team with authentic local knowledge and community connections
- Underserved market (business visitors vs. leisure tourists)
- Low capital investment needed (leverage existing assets)
- Scalable through partnerships rather than hiring
- The G20 Summit helped validate the market opportunity and gave the team a concrete catalyst for developing the ACE concept

### Key Risks
- Seasonality (conference calendar, business travel patterns)
- Safety perceptions of Johannesburg
- Quality control with community partners
- Competition from established tour operators
- Economic sensitivity (corporate travel budgets)
- Dependency on key individuals (Praveen, Thandi's grandmother)

### Success Metrics (Year 1)
- 100 experiences delivered
- 5 hotel partnerships secured
- 3 corporate accounts (repeat bookings)
- 20+ community/venue partnerships established
- Net Promoter Score >70
- Average review rating >4.5/5
- Break-even by Month 6
