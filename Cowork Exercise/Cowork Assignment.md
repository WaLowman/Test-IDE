# COWORK ASSIGNMENT

## Stage 1 - Business Model Canvas

A structured BMC Report developed interactively through all 9 Building Blocks of the Business Model Canvas framework for Highveld-GTS's Authentic Cultural Experiences business line. Each building block is:

- Presented individually and approved before proceeding to the next
- Specific and business-like with quantitative estimates (pricing ranges, volume assumptions, cost benchmarks)
- Aligned to the standard BMC names: Customer Segments, Value Propositions, Channels, Customer Relationships, Revenue Streams, Key Resources, Key Activities, Key Partnerships, Cost Structure

## Stage 2 - Financial Model Input Parameters

A structured markdown document that extracts all financial parameters from the BMC Report needed to build a 5-year Excel cash flow model. At minimum, identify:

- Revenue streams and pricing assumptions
- Customer/market size estimates and growth rates
- Cost structure (fixed vs. variable costs)
- Capital expenditure requirements
- Key financial assumptions (inflation, discount rates, margins)
- Sensitivity drivers and gaps flagged for further research

## Stage 3 - Financial Model Spreadsheet

A standalone Excel spreadsheet with a 5-year cash flow forecast horizon, treating the business line as a standalone operation. Key features:

- Assumptions sheet as single control panel with named ranges and input-formatted cells
- Revenue and Cost build-up sheets with quarterly detail (20 periods)
- Cashflow Forecast with cumulative position, breakeven highlight, NPV and IRR
- Scenario Analysis with Best/Base/Worst case selector driving all sheets
- Low-confidence assumptions marked for later validation via Claude for Excel
