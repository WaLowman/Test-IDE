## Sandton Convention Centre

### Snapshot

- slug: sandton-convention-centre
- pillar: clients
- category: conference venue
- location: Sandton
- website or source: https://sandtoncentral.co.za/sandton-convention-centre

### Fit

- The venue positions itself as Africa's premier exhibition, conference, and events destination.
- Its Sandton location, proximity to Gautrain and OR Tambo, and surrounding hotel cluster make it ideal for pre-event, post-event, spouse, and VIP side programmes.

### Route-In

- Best route-in is through reservations, events sales, or venue management rather than generic front desk.
- Visible public contact includes `SCC.reservations@southernsun.com` and main line `011 779 0000`.

### Evidence

- Official venue copy highlights `22,000 m²` of flexible event space and capacity from small business meetings to major conferences.
- The venue explicitly markets accessibility for international delegates and location inside Sandton's business district.
- Conference demand is structurally ongoing, not seasonal.

### Offer Angle

- Position as a low-friction delegate enhancement partner: half-day Johannesburg heritage orientation, executive spouse programme, or curated cultural dinner transfer package.
- Emphasise punctual transport, controlled logistics, and culturally credible hosting for international visitors with limited time.

### Risks

- Procurement could run through event organisers, hotels, or agencies rather than SCC directly.
- Large venue teams may prefer established DMC relationships.

### Recommended Move

- Send a concise intro asking who handles delegate social programmes and off-site guest experiences, with one premium sample itinerary attached.

## Radisson Blu Gautrain Hotel Sandton Johannesburg

- slug: radisson-blu-gautrain-hotel-sandton-johannesburg
- pillar: clients
- category: hotel
- location: Sandton
- website or source: https://www.radissonhotels.com/en-us/hotels/radisson-blu-johannesburg-sandton-gautrain/services

### Fit

- The hotel is built around business travel and convenience, with concierge service, executive business lounge, airport shuttle, and meeting facilities.
- That combination makes it a practical buyer for premium add-on guest experiences rather than only room nights.

### Route-In

- Best route-in is concierge, guest relations, front office management, or meetings/events sales.
- Public details include `reservations.gautrain@radissonblu.com` and `+27 (11) 286 1000`.

### Evidence

- The official services page highlights concierge, meeting facilities, executive business lounge, and airport shuttle.
- The property is marketed as being in the city's business district and close to airport-linked transport.
- This indicates a steady flow of short-stay international guests with free pockets of time.

### Offer Angle

- Offer a small set of hotel-ready experiences: `3-hour executive Johannesburg orientation`, `hosted Maboneng art and dinner evening`, and `Soweto culture half-day with hotel pickup`.
- Emphasise easy handoff to concierge and predictable timing.

### Risks

- Hotels often protect guest trust and may be cautious about outside suppliers.
- Commission expectations or supplier approval may slow activation.

### Recommended Move

- Approach concierge and guest-relations with a simple hotel-partner deck and ask to pilot one vetted package for business travellers staying two nights or more.

- slug: tantor-relocation-services

- location: Gauteng
- website or source: https://tantor.co.za/

### Fit

- Tantor already sells destination services including area tours, cultural coaching, home finding, and settling-in support.
- That makes them a direct commercial fit rather than an educational prospect.

### Route-In

- Best route-in is founder or relocation consultant level.
- Public details include `meryn@tantor.co.za` and a clear relocation-services positioning.

### Evidence

- The site explicitly lists `Area Tour`, `Cultural Coaching`, `Destination Services`, and support for international assignments.
- It states that the company supports corporate organisations through relocation processes in South Africa.
- This signals both buyer authority and strong audience overlap.

### Offer Angle

- Position as a specialist local execution partner for premium Johannesburg orientation days and curated cultural settling-in experiences for assignees and spouses.
- Emphasise safety, professionalism, and flexibility by client nationality and time window.

### Risks

- Tantor may already have incumbent suppliers.
- Their existing area tour product could mean they want white-label support rather than branded co-selling.

### Recommended Move

- Lead with a white-label capable offer and ask whether they need overflow capacity, premium upgrades, or more culturally differentiated orientation experiences.
