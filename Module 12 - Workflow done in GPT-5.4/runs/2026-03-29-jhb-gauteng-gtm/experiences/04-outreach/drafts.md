## Internal Sales Prep Note: Executive Johannesburg Heritage Orientation

### Target

Internal sales and partnership use

### Objective

Prepare one flagship short-format product that hotels, chambers, and conference teams can understand in under one minute.

### Notes

- Core promise: `serious Johannesburg context in half a day, without logistical headache`.
- Primary buyer: concierge, event planner, relocation consultant, executive assistant.
- Primary audience: first-time international business visitor with `3 to 5` free hours.
- Anchor stops: Constitution Hill and either a shortened Apartheid Museum segment or one additional city-context stop depending on timing.
- Operational rule: pickup and drop-off from Sandton or Rosebank only for the first version.
- Sales language should avoid `tour`; prefer `hosted orientation` or `curated half-day programme`.
