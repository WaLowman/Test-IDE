# Business Context

## Working Business Definition

A Johannesburg tour and transport business offering curated cultural experiences for high-trust, time-constrained international visitors in Gauteng.

## Offer Shape

The business sells combinations of:

- airport and city transport
- executive-safe hosted movement
- cultural immersion with context
- half-day and full-day curated itineraries
- concierge-style handling for VIPs, executives, diplomats, and hosted groups

## Best-Fit Demand Signals

- inbound business travel
- diplomatic visits
- visiting executive programs
- conference or summit side-programming
- delegation hosting
- relocation support for international arrivals
- premium leisure add-ons attached to business trips

## Likely Proof Points To Emphasise

- reliability and punctuality
- Johannesburg and Gauteng local knowledge
- culturally credible curation
- comfort and safety
- easy planning for assistants, hosts, and programme managers
- flexible transport plus experience packaging

## Default Market Scope

- Johannesburg
- Sandton
- Rosebank
- Pretoria and Tshwane when relevant
- broader Gauteng for selected experiences and client access

## Commercial Pillars

## Clients

Direct buyers or internal sponsors who may purchase experiences for guests, teams, delegates, or VIP visitors.

Examples:

- embassies and consulates
- chambers of commerce
- conference organisers
- destination management companies
- relocation firms
- corporate executive offices
- hotels with concierge or guest relations teams

## Partners

Intermediaries or ecosystem players who can refer, resell, bundle, host, or enable the offer.

Examples:

- boutique hotels
- event agencies
- private guides
- transport operators with gaps in cultural programming
- premium venues
- tour desks
- international business networks

## Experiences

Sellable curated products or provider stacks that can become signature offerings.

Examples:

- Soweto culture and history hosted route
- Johannesburg art and design circuit
- apartheid history and civic context program
- food-focused cultural evening
- diplomacy-safe executive city orientation
- Gauteng day programme combining transport, hosting, and curated stops
