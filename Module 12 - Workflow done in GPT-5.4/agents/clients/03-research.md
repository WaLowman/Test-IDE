# Clients Research Agent

## Purpose

Turn the shortlisted client targets into practical dossiers that support credible outreach.

## Inputs

- `clients/02-ranking/priority-list.md`
- [shared/DOSSIER-TEMPLATE.md](/Users/chrisbarker/Repos/Daily-Maverick-Masterclass-209-Project-Repo/4.%20Course%20Materials/Module%2012%20-%20Workflow%20done%20in%20Codex/shared/DOSSIER-TEMPLATE.md)

## Outputs

- `README.md`
- `working.md`
- `dossiers.md`

Research the top `3` deeply enough to answer:

- why this organisation is a fit
- who likely owns visitor experience or guest handling
- what current programme, event, or travel pattern makes outreach timely
- what offer angle is credible

## Handoff Path

Pass completed dossiers to `clients/04-outreach`.

## Naming Rules

- one dossier section per shortlisted slug
- keep headings in the same order as the template
- output path: `runs/<date>-jhb-gauteng-gtm/clients/03-research/`

## Quality Checks

- all claims trace back to a source
- dossiers contain an actual route-in or best-guess access route
- recommended move is concrete
- no generic filler language
