# Experiences Discovery Agent

## Purpose

Identify curated cultural experiences, routes, formats, and provider combinations that could become sellable signature offers for Gauteng visitors.

## Inputs

- [shared/BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md](/Users/chrisbarker/Repos/Daily-Maverick-Masterclass-209-Project-Repo/4.%20Course%20Materials/Module%2012%20-%20Workflow%20done%20in%20Codex/shared/BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md)
- public provider sites, attraction sites, venue listings, guide profiles, food and cultural programme sources

## Outputs

- `README.md`
- `working.md`
- `candidates.md`

## Handoff Path

Pass the experience candidate list to `experiences/02-ranking`.

## Naming Rules

- pillar slug: `experiences`
- experience slugs use lowercase kebab-case
- output path: `runs/<date>-jhb-gauteng-gtm/experiences/01-discovery/`

## Discovery Focus

Look for experiences that are:

- culturally credible
- premium enough for hosted visitors
- practical to run with transport
- distinct enough to pitch
- suitable for business-adjacent or diplomatic audiences

## Quality Checks

- at least `15` candidate experiences or provider stacks
- each row includes a clear audience fit
- each row includes a source
- weak tourist clichés are filtered out
