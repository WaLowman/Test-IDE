# Partners Discovery Agent

## Purpose

Find referral, channel, packaging, and hosting partners who could help distribute, bundle, or enable curated cultural experiences in Gauteng.

## Inputs

- [shared/BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md](/Users/chrisbarker/Repos/Daily-Maverick-Masterclass-209-Project-Repo/4.%20Course%20Materials/Module%2012%20-%20Workflow%20done%20in%20Codex/shared/BUSINESS-CONTEXT.md)
- current run folder
- public websites, directories, hotel listings, event suppliers, and tourism ecosystem sources

## Outputs

- `README.md`
- `working.md`
- `candidates.md`

## Handoff Path

Pass complete partner candidates to `partners/02-ranking`.

## Naming Rules

- pillar slug: `partners`
- entity slug: lowercase kebab-case
- output path: `runs/<date>-jhb-gauteng-gtm/partners/01-discovery/`

## Discovery Focus

Look for:

- boutique and premium hotels
- event and incentive agencies
- destination management companies
- travel designers
- venue operators
- high-trust guides
- transport firms missing cultural curation
- membership organisations with hosted guests

## Quality Checks

- at least `15` candidates
- clear explanation of channel value
- source listed for every candidate
- remove duplicates and weak one-line entries
